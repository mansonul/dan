Safe, comfortable, eco-friendly, helpful and classy, The Bear’s Van is a 2016 Renault Trafic Phase III built and maintained as the perfect vehicle for small groups.

Safety:

The van is rated 4 out of 5 stars in the EURO NCAP safety tests

Comfort:

The 6 seats in the back plus the 2 others in front are built with ergonomics and comfort of the passengers in mind. Air conditioning and heating with passenger control are also big bonuses, either in the heat of summer or the cold snowy winters and everything in between.

Eco-friendly:

The EURO6 small diesel engine tuned to provide enough power for an 8 people carrier is also fitted with the new AdBlue technology, which reduces CO2 emissions drastically.
Certified as ECO2 by ISO14001 standards, this van is built with more than 11% recycled plastics and 95% of it is built with recyclable materials.

Helpful:

The interior of the van is designed to accommodate your travel experience perfectly: side pockets for various things to keep handy, reading lights, USB charger plugs and a large storage space in the back will make your ride as smooth as it could be.

Classy:

Together with the new sleek design that Renault has been constantly improving since they first launched the Trafic line of vans in the 80’s, the white colour and the shaded rear windows add a touch of style and comfort as well as privacy, which is especially useful while travelling through crowded touristic places.

Free wi-fi:

We know how difficult it can be to keep connected while travelling through time zones and roaming legislation so we took care of that for you, even through the mountains. Free wi-fi will be available for the whole duration of your tour.
