The Bear

Locals make the best guides because they have lived and experienced the sites that you end up visiting.

It is very rare, though, to find a guide that has actually lived the castle experience as an insider.

Having played concerts and medieval feasts with knights and ladies of the court, with ales and wines and foods of older times, with costumes and swords and traditional instruments, The Bear was privileged enough to gather stories like no others. From playing barefoot on tables to courting beautiful damsels, you will get the chance to meet a real life medieval party animal.

Frontman of Ad Hoc - the only transylvanian medieval acoustic punk band in the world - hitchhiker, street musician, motorhome dweller, band tour manager, roadie, off-grid enthusiast, nature lover, mushroom hunter, fisherman, archer and just generally a wanderer, The Bear brings a unique flavour to any tour. Whether it’s the palinka, the stories, his jolliness, his lust for life or all of the above, your host for Bear Tours is a destination himself.

“What I do know is that this suits me better than anything.”

“My whole life has been connected to travelling, from an early age when my parents would take me and my brother camping throughout all of Romania, to my adult years.

The happiest moments I remember have always been in a car, with pleasant company, supplies and a tank full of fuel. Whether it’s the freedom of the open road, the speed of churning kilometers under your wheels, the lack of responsibility that comes from being in-between, I never could decide what makes it so magical for me.

After about 10 years as a professional musician, when my band fell apart I was left with an abundance of magical memories from most of the amazing historical sites of Romania as well as the most party-oriented and wild nightlife cities of this corrupt but absolutely magical country that is Romania. I was lucky enough to play my modern yet traditional music barefoot and dressed almost as a superhero in ancient hallways and on castle walls, to see these sites without any tourists cluttering them or even party, sleep and “court” ladies as in the times of old, as our forefathers did once.

My most treasured memories and my jolly experience are what I aim to share with everybody through Bear Tours. I am not a sterile tour guide that has been taught about history in an online class. I have lived, understood and contributed myself to the history of these magical places that we could visit together.

From the Dracula’s Transylvania Tour to my off-grid middle of the forest camps, my plan is to live adventures with potential friends, not simply visit some sites and tick a box on a map. From the chili pepper tattoo on my right middle finger to my own spicy version of the traditional romanian plum distillation called palinka, to the thousands of people that have blessed me by listening to my music, my plan with Bear Tours is to put them all in and make this new adventure a magical way of life.“
